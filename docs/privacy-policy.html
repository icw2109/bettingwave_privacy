Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-11-14 · BettingWave

1. Overview

BettingWave ("the App") provides AI-powered matching of public news to public prediction markets. We do not collect personally identifiable information ("PII") to deliver core functionality.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Financial account data
  • Precise or coarse location
  • Contacts or calendar
  • Device identifiers for profiling

4. Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, the operating system may generate a device token. This token is used solely to deliver alerts you opt into (e.g., flagged market matches). We do not combine tokens with personal profiles.

5. Third-Party Services

The backend calls external APIs for news (RSS, GNews) and market data (Kalshi, Polymarket). These services may have their own privacy policies. We do not forward any user identifiers to them.

6. Analytics & Tracking

No in-app analytics are currently implemented. Crash reporting and basic telemetry may be collected by the OS or app store distribution channel in aggregated form.

7. Data Security

Server infrastructure runs in isolated containers with limited-retention caches. No user secrets are stored. App-to-server communication may use HTTP or HTTPS depending on deployment; we recommend and are migrating to HTTPS to protect transport integrity.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under 13. No personal data collection occurs.

9. Financial & Investment Disclaimer

Content surfaced by the App is informational only and should not be considered financial advice. Users are responsible for their own decisions.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy for operational or legal reasons. The Last Updated date will be revised accordingly.
